# 🚀 SkillSync AI

### AI-Powered Career Intelligence & Employability Platform

SkillSync AI is an AI-driven career intelligence platform designed to help students, freshers, and job seekers become industry-ready professionals through personalized analysis, career guidance, and skill development recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Millions of students struggle with:

- Lack of career clarity
- Poor ATS resume scores
- Skill gaps between academics and industry
- Random learning without direction
- Limited access to personalized mentorship
- Difficulty finding relevant internships and opportunities

As a result, many talented students remain underprepared for real-world careers.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

SkillSync AI bridges the gap between education and industry by providing:

- AI Resume Analysis
- ATS Score Optimization
- Skill Gap Detection
- Career Readiness Assessment
- Personalized Learning Roadmaps
- Internship & Hackathon Recommendations
- Certification Suggestions
- Career Growth Insights
